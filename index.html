Similarity-Aware Channel pruning

Proposed a novel channel pruning method to accelerate and compress CNNs, allowing deployment on resource-constrained devices. The method compares similarity of a layer's output feature maps based on five criteria. This approach is particularly useful when deploying CNNs on devices with limited resources, such as mobile phones or IoT devices, where the reduced size and faster processing can be critical for efficient operation.
